03 · THE STAKES

HALF NEVER DO.

One hundred golfers tee off. About half never sign a card that starts with a 9. The line you are trying to cross is the median, not the tour.

No swing change is required to cross it. No new driver. The members who cross carry nothing the others don’t — except a budget.

THE TURN · 06 · THE HUNDRED–YARD RELIGION

You don’t need 14 clubs. You need one number you trust.

Scores are made inside 100 yards. Build one repeatable full‑wedge number and lay up to it on purpose — 100 yards of certainty beats 40 yards of guesswork. The target is always the middle of the green. The middle is thirty feet from everywhere.

100y OF CERTAINTY > 40y OF GUESSWORK
